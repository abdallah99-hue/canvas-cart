# MyArtSpace

**MyArtSpace** is a dedicated online platform designed specifically for talented young artists to showcase their creative works.  
This platform allows artists to present their paintings, digital artworks, and other creative pieces to a wider audience.  

At the same time, MyArtSpace connects art lovers, collectors, and buyers with these emerging artists, making it easy to discover, explore, and support original art.  
Our goal is to create a thriving community where creativity meets opportunity, and where both artists and art enthusiasts can benefit.  

Users can explore the platform to find unique artworks, learn about the artists behind them, and purchase pieces securely online.  
The platform emphasizes **user experience, responsive design, and accessibility**, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the artworks on any device, from desktop computers to mobile phones.  

---

## Key Features

- **Gallery Browsing**: Explore a wide collection of artworks from talented young artists.  
- **Artwork Details**: Click on any artwork to view detailed information including the artist’s name, medium, and price.  
- **Personal Collection / Cart**: Add artworks to your personal collection or shopping cart for easy access and checkout.  
- **Secure Purchases**: Buy artworks safely and securely with confidence.  
- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices.  
- **Interactive Interface**: Smooth animations, easy navigation, and a modern user experience.  

---

## Technologies Used

- **Vite** – for fast development and optimized build.  
- **React** – for building interactive user interfaces.  
- **TypeScript** – for type-safe JavaScript development.  
- **Tailwind CSS** – for modern and responsive styling.  
- **shadcn-ui** – for prebuilt UI components and better design consistency.
